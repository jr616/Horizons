Inspiration

1 Break In every 15 seconds.

Are you in student houseing?

Do you need to lock a door without causing lasting damage?

Introducing the all new Stop and Lock, a quick and damage free solution for all your locking needs.

Feeling secure should no be a privilege that only those who own there own home can experianc. There are thousands of people in the UK living in shared rented accommodation and often there are no locks on their bedroom doors. Landlords can refuse to fit a permanent lock as it will damage the door. Team 'Bob the Builder' have decided to fix this issue, with the first secure lock that requires no screws, nails or glue.


A CAD render
of the current design

The Stop and Lock will easily slide under any door
and can be locked or unlocked via an app or a mechanical key.

Domain Name

Setting Up Domain Name

Find avalible and fitting domain on a registrar - in this case 'GoDaddy' was used, the largest registrar in the world. A registrar has premission from the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers (ICANN) to manage and sell domain names.

Pay for the domain name for at least one year, giving you alone the right to publish information to the internet thought this domain. In this case, the name 'stopandlock.co.uk' cost £0.99 for one year.

Connect the domain with the website by editing the DNS (domain name settings) on the registrar and GitHub
